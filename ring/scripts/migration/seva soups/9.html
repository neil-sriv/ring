How has your definition of happiness changed as you've gotten older?

Irika: yes - i think defined a lot more by the people in my life

Ranjan : As I've gotten older, my definition of happiness has shifted. When I was younger, I used to think it was all about achieving big goals and acquiring stuff. Now, I find happiness in simpler things like spending quality time with loved ones, enjoying a good meal, or just having a peaceful moment with myself. It's more about balance and appreciating the little things in life.

Riya: I've learned that chasing high highs can come with low lows, so I'm finding that happiness is more contentment and consistently being somewhere in the middle.

It’s also hard not to compare your status with peers and colleagues, so I think my definition hasn’t changed but I now have the context on how hard it is to maintain
