Anjali Asarpota asked: What do you take for granted?

Ranjan : My friends and family

Irika: I think I take my time with my family for granted. The little two-minute Facetimes with my dad on my walks home from work, scattered moments that I get with my mom, and sometimes unanswered texts from my brother.

I think I historically have taken time with my family for granted and I've never been one to ever really be homesick. But the realization that I will likely never again live at home for more than a few weeks at a time for the rest of my life is pretty spooky scary and sad. So I think maybe this is becoming more of an answer to "what have you taken for granted?" and not "what do you take for granted?" because day by day I'm beginning to learn to be more intentional with the time I spend with my family and not just dismiss the moments I get with them.
